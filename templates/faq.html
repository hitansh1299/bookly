Frequently Ask Questions

General Questions

How do I put an ad?

For putting an ad, you need to be a registered user of Bookly. If you don't have an account you can sign up by clicking on the Login/Register Tab in the top right corner. After logging in, you need to simply click on "Post Free Ad" and fill out the necessary details. You can edit/view the status of your Ads >My Ads.

Where can I see the status of my ad?

You can check the status of your ad in {username}>My Ads.

How can I edit information on my Ad?

Go to {username}>My Ads then find the ad you want to edit and click on edit to modify your ad or delete to erase ad; this will delete your post.

How do I contact the seller to buy?

You can contact them via the mail id they have given which is displayed on the right of the ad.

Is there a limit to the number of ads I can post?

No, you can post as many ads as you like.
