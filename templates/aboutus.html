About Us

How we Started

Every year, students from Schools and Colleges around the globe promote to a higher academic level and no longer require some of their previous year books. So many books every year are thrown away, recycled or just merely kept at home piling dust while there are so many around us who could make good use of the book and are in dire need of them. However, not always do we happen to know someone who needs them. Bookly was started with the intention of connecting all of us- the ones who can help and the ones who need it. We wanted to create a platform where we as a society can be there for one another and help the needy by selling our books that are no longer required at a minimal or low cost.

Vision

“We can’t help everyone, but everyone can help someone” Trying to do our bit, we started with a simple objective of connecting us by providing a platform which is an opportunity to those who can help, and a help for those who need it. We, at Bookly believe books are our wealth, and that to have them be of no use is to destroy its value, Bookly is a platform where you can buy and sell books at minimal prices.

How we work

Bookly is a modern day responsive website that is designed as a platform for users to buy and sell books online. With an intuitive UI and a large collection of books, one can quickly and easily find the books they're looking for. It is built using the Flask microframework as the backend and HTML 5, CSS, JavaScript and Bootstrap for the front end. It provides users classification by subjects and age-group to facilitate a quick search. It also provides a dashboard service to the seller so that they can view and update the status of their ads
